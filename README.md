# 🏠 RenovateAI — AI Home Renovation Agent

**Powered by Nano Banana Pro**

An AI-powered single-page web application that helps you visualize, plan, and budget home renovations. Upload a photo of any room, choose a design style, and see your space transformed instantly.

## ✨ Features

- **Room Analysis** — Upload a photo and get instant AI-powered room detection, condition assessment, and renovation suggestions.
- **Style Selector** — Choose from 8 curated design styles (Modern, Industrial, Scandinavian, Bohemian, Japandi, Coastal, Art Deco, Mid-Century Modern).
- **Before & After** — Drag the interactive slider to compare original vs. AI-renovated room views.
- **Cost Estimator** — Adjust room size, renovation depth, and material quality sliders for instant cost breakdowns.
- **AI Chat Assistant** — Ask the AI about design ideas, costs, color palettes, and more.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser.
2. Explore the features, upload a room photo, and chat with the AI assistant.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5** + **CSS3** + **Vanilla JavaScript**
- **Google Fonts**: Outfit, Inter
- Custom glassmorphism design with micro-animations
- No frameworks, no dependencies, no build step
